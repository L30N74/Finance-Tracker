# Finance-Tracker

An app to help me keep track of my spendings on a monthly basis.

## How to use

When opening the app for the first time, and when opening it for the first time each month, The app will ask how much money you have available for the month.

After that you will be redirected to the homescreen where you can see your the amount of money you started the month with, the amount of money you spent so far and how much of your available money is left.

## Adding some expenses!

Click on the Plus button and fill out the form. There you can create your own groups to sort expenses into if so desired.

## Filter

Expenses can be filtered/sorted on the homescreen. When filtering for Income, no expenses will be shown and vise versa. 

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/51761671/122686889-e39f1f80-d213-11eb-96f0-8665aacc0db2.jpg" width=250>

## Graph-View

In this view you can see how much money you had available each month, how much you spent and how much of the available money you have saved up.
When clicking on an entry, a detailed view over all expenses that took place in that month will be shown.
